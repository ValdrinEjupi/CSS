Gradient Colors

Linear Gradients

background-image:linear-gradient(red,yellow) it start from up to down

background-image:linear-gradient(to right,red,yellow) from left to right

background-image:linear-gradient(to bottom right,red,yellow) for diagonally start from up left to bottom right

background-image:linear-gradient(to right,rgba(255,0,0,0),rgba(255,0,0,1)) we can use also rgba values

background-image:linear-gradient(red,yellow 10%,green 20%) we can also use %-percentage for colors

background-image:repeating-linear-gradient(red,yellow 10%,green 15%)

Radial Gradients

background-image:radial-gradient(red,yellow,green) - by default shape eshte elips dhe fillon nga brenda per jasht

background-color:radial-gradient(red 5%,yellow 15%,green 60%)-edhe ketu mundemi me perdor perqidje per ngjyrat

background-color:radiant-color(circle,red,yellow,green)-ketu mundemi me ndryshu edhe formen qe tham by default osht eclipse

background-image:radial-gradient(closest-side at 60% 55%,red,yellow,black)

background-image:radial-gradient(farthest-side at 60% 55%,red,yellow,black)

background-image:radial-gradient(closest-corner at 60% 55%,red,yellow,black)

background-image:radial-gradient(farthest-corner at 60% 55%,red,yellow,black)
